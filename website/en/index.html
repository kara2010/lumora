Why the streaming is special

  • Up to 4K / 60 fps / 25 Mbit/s constant – encoded by your GPU's hardware encoder (NVIDIA, AMD, Intel), barely touching game performance.
  • Direct peer-to-peer: video and audio travel straight from your PC to your viewers. No vendor server that recompresses your stream or reads your content.
  • Viewers need nothing but a link – no app, no account. Group rooms show everyone's stream in a grid, iPhone included.
  • Adaptive bitrate: if a viewer is on weak mobile data, Lumora steps the bitrate down automatically – and back up when the connection recovers.
  • Game-only audio: when sharing a single window, only that game's sound is transmitted – your music, calls and notifications stay private.
  • Zero router fiddling: connection setup is automatic (UPnP, IPv6 direct path for DS-Lite/CGNAT connections). The public link is ready in about two seconds.
