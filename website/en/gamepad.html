Couch PC gaming: one launcher, all your games, fully gamepad-controlled

Gaming PC hooked up to the TV – but you still grab mouse and keyboard just to launch a game? Not with Lumora: the entire interface is fully controllable with an Xbox gamepad, from the cover library down to every single setting.

⬇ Download Lumora for free

For Windows 10 & 11 · no registration · open source

Built for the couch

  • All stores in one library: Steam, Epic, GOG, Ubisoft, Xbox/Game Pass, EA, Battle.net, Rockstar, Amazon and Riot – a beautiful cover wall with search, sorting and favorites.
  • Real gamepad navigation: spatial movement across the tile grid, row-aware navigation in dialogs, right stick scrolls, focus ring always visible, live preview while browsing.
  • Freely bindable hotkeys – including gamepad combos (even with the Xbox Guide button): bring Lumora to the front, toggle the OSD, or start streaming the game you're playing – without leaving it.
  • Automatic HDR: switches on per game at launch and back off afterwards – no more digging through Windows menus.
  • Playtime tracking & recap: total time, most played, favorite genre – nicely presented.
  • OSD & streaming included: FPS/hardware overlay in-game and 4K streaming to friends – both gamepad-controllable too.

Why this makes evenings easier

Reaching for the mouse is the moment the couch evening breaks. Lumora can start minimized with Windows, comes to the front on a gamepad combo, and from there the controller stays in your hand: pick a game, play, pick the next. Covers and descriptions load automatically – the library looks great even on a big TV.
